--- title: The Hapstrument year: 2022 place: Waterloo, Canada thumbnail: collab: - Derrek Chow - Jonathan Lane-Smith - Sahand Ajami text: We present the Hapstrument, a novel bimanual haptic device for musical expression. Composed of two Haply 2diy haptic force feedback devices, the instrument allows a user to control pitch with one hand, and excitation with the other. We evaluated the Hapstrument's playability in an informal study with musicians and validated our original design goal through a live performance with the Hapstrument.

The Hapstrument was a result of the graduate level course CanHap501: Introduction to Haptic Information Design in Winter 2022 taught by top haptic researchers in Canada as part of CanHaptics network. external: publications: - authors: Jonathan Lane-Smith, Derrek Chow, Sahand Ajami, Jeremy Cooperstock venue: NIME 2023 --- {% include media.html youtube="https://www.youtube.com/embed/wla1DojQy9w" %} {% include media.html img="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/17170744/154556946-13dc53a2-b280-407d-b2c2-2a6e68a1ebc5.jpg"%} {% include media.html img="https://dl.acm.org/cms/attachment/d702e3a0-a533-495f-a7e1-f9618d48b9b6/icmi21-123-fig2.jpg" %}

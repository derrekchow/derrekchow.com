A hackathon is an event that challenges students to experiment and create unique software or hardware projects from scratch. Participants collaborate in small teams and compete over an short and intense period of time, typically 24 to 48 hours. Featured are a selection of award-winning hackathon projects constructed with found materials. Hundreds (to thousands) of students around the world to.

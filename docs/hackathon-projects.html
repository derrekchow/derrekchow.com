Hackathon Projects

A hackathon is an event that challenges students to experiment and create unique software or hardware projects from scratch. Hundreds to thousands of students collaborate in small teams and compete over a short and intense time period, typically 24 to 48 hours. Below are selected award-winning hackathon projects created with found materials.

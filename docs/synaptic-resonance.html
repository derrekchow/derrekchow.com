Synaptic Resonance

|



Synaptic Resonance is an interactive light and sound installation. Debuted at Burning Man 2022, participants lay on a vibrating platform under neuron inspired LED lights immersed in an ambisonic sound experience. The 3D mapped LEDs play animations synced with the 3D audio as well as the vibrations below.

Exhibition

Burning Man 2022. Camp Synesthesia. Black Rock City, Nevada, United States. Aug 28 - Sep 5, 2022.

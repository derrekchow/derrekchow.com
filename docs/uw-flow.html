UW Flow

|



UW Flow is a popular platform for University of Waterloo students to share course and professor reviews. It was created in 2012 by a group of software engineering students. While the site remained a useful resource for students, it began to suffer over time from lack of maintenance. In 2019, we decided to revamp UW Flow with an entirely new design and codebase.

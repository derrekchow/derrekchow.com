About

Derrek Chow is an inventor and artist exploring how computers can enable new forms of interaction and experience. Using design and technology as tools of inquiry, he creates experiential works in various forms such as artifact, installation, and performance.

Derrek received a degree in software engineering from the University of Waterloo where he studied in the Expressive Input & Interaction Group. He was a designer at RNDR and a Human-Computer Interaction researcher at the ACME Lab in the ATLAS Institute and the NTU HCI Lab in National Taiwan University.

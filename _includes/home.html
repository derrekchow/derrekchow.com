About

Derrek Chow is an inventor and artist from Toronto, Canada. He explores news forms of interaction with computers through making. Using design and technology as tools of inquiry, he creates highly experiential works that sits between artifact, installation, performance, and more.

Derrek conducted research in Human-Computer Interaction at the ACME Lab in the ATLAS Institute and the NTU HCI Lab in National Taiwan University, and was as a designer at RNDR.
